About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineer with a strong background in Mechatronics, Automation and Industrial Robotics. I have a strong understanding of engineering principles and excel at problem solving. I have experience across mechanical design, robotics, manufacturing systems, and simulation, I thrive at the intersection of hardware and software. Robotics are no longer a mere subject of science fiction novels, movies and TV shows. They already coexist with people in various settings, albeit not in the form sci-fi stories used to depict them. With recent investment and advances in technology, A.I. has reached a new milestone. Advancements in digital A.I. pave the way forward for innovations in physical AI (which are Robots!). Collaborative robots reflects the focus of the industry towards creating robots that are simpler, easier to program, and able to integrate into current processes.

Looking ahead, I aim to contribute to organizations that are shaping the future of manufacturing, automation, and robotics—where I can push the boundaries of what machines can do and help bring smart, sustainable, and high-performance systems to life while working with cross-functional teams or independently driving innovation.

I’ve worked with tools like SolidWorks, ANSYS, MATLAB, and ROS, and I enjoy bringing ideas to life through CAD, FEA, and embedded systems. Whether it’s designing smarter manufacturing workflows or simulating robotic arms for precision tasks, I’m always looking to build, iterate, and improve.

Let’s build something great—together.
