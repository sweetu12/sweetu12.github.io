Oxitech: Modular Water Oxidation System

This is our final capstone project, which was sponsored by two companies: TechnIEQ and Oxitec. We were tasked with making a downsized, scalable, and modular Pulse-Oxidation system that is easy to maintain and has future upgrade compatibility for communication technologies using WiFi and PLC/HMI Systems.

This is the initial prototype design [PHASE 1]

The above design was then modified, manufactured, assembled, and tested in a real environment to get test data [PHASE 2].

These scalable and modular enclosures house a Pulse-Oxidation mechanism that utilizes dissolved ozone and activated oxygen species in water as a natural disinfectant, facilitated by patented Oxi-Cells within the enclosure. This design is highly scalable and modular, as you can connect 200+ cells to a single hub, which means we can connect multiple of these 4-cell modules in series for large-scale applications. It's also easy to disassemble and maintain.
