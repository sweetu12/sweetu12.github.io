PLC Projects

My experience of PLCs comes from my University Lab classes and a theory class about the inner workings and architecture of a PLC and the communication protocols, HMI, SCADA, etc. I have experience with Bosch Rexroth and Seimens PLC.

Buffer Store Simulation (Ladder Logic) (Simulated in Factory.io)

Problem: In an assembly line, there is buffer storage for valve blocks. The addition and removal of the valve blocks are controlled by the light barriers B1 and B2, the impulses of which are supplied to a counter. If the inventory reaches the upper limit of 30 units, the conveyor belt is to be switched off. If the upper limit is undershot, it must be switched on again immediately. If the inventory undershoots the lower limit value of 10 units, this must be indicated by the LED H2. The counter is calibrated (reset to storage content zero) at the beginning of the shift with an empty magazine using the S5 pushbutton. The system is switched on using the S1 pushbutton "Start" and switched off using the S2 pushbutton "Stop".

Sorting by Height with S7-1200 (PLC-Sim)

Two different types of boxes (Tall box & Short box) enter a sorting system at random in a line on a conveyor belt. The sensor scans the height of the box, and then the box is sorted according to its height by a multi-directional conveyor, which sends it towards the left or to the right, thus sorting them effectively.
