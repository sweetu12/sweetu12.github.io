PLC Projects

My experience of PLCs comes from my University Lab classes and a theory class about the inner workings and architecture of a PLC and the communication protocols, HMI, SCADA, etc. I have experience with Bosch Rexroth and Seimens PLC. Programming experience in Ladder Logic (LD), Structured Text (ST), and Siemens SCL. Use of Timers, counters, logical/arithmetic operators, DAQ, File/data operators, etc.

Pick and Place on S7-1200

Programmed in Ladder logic (LD), SCL, ST. Simulated a Pick and Place mechanism on a Siemens S7-1200 PLC with Factory.io and Siemens PLCSIM, Programmed in the Siemens Totally Integrated Automation (TIA) Portal. The items on the conveyor belt are automatically picked up by the robot following input from the sensor and transferred to another belt.

Sorting by Height with S7-1200 (Siemens TIA Portal & PLC-Sim)

Programmed in Ladder logic (LD). Two different types of boxes (Tall box & Short box) enter a sorting system at random in a line on a conveyor belt. The sensor scans the height of the box, and then the box is sorted according to its height by a multi-directional conveyor, which sends it towards the left or to the right, thus sorting them effectively.
